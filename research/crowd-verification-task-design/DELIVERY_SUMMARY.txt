CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS (Absolute Musts)
================================================================================

1. CONTEXT in every task (show why verification matters)
2. 3-PERSON CONSENSUS (prevent single-person error)
3. HONEYPOTS every 10 tasks (catch gaming)
4. REAL-TIME FEEDBACK (users know if they're right)
5. ESCALATION PATH (expert review for disagreement)
6. NO FINANCIAL INCENTIVES (reputation only, money kills quality)
7. MOBILE-FIRST UI (single tap decisions)

ALL SEVEN are required for success. Missing even one significantly reduces engagement.
