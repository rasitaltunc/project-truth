## MAJOR INSIGHTS BY CATEGORY

### Community Models
- **Best:** Bellingcat's 100+ volunteers (mission-aligned, self-sustaining)
- **Largest:** ICIJ's 200-journalist consortium (mutual benefit from coordinated investigations)
- **Direct:** OCCRP's 50-outlet partnerships (relationship-based, staff-intensive)
- **Lesson:** Community is not feature; it's business model

### Funding Patterns
- **Risky:** OCCRP's 50%+ US government dependency
- **Successful:** Bellingcat's 50% grants + 30% training revenue + 20% in-kind
- **Lesson:** Diversification = editorial independence

### Verification Approaches
- **Risky:** Pure AI verification (hallucination problems)
- **Proven:** ICIJ's Automatic + Manual + Peer review
- **Proven:** Bellingcat's Methodology transparency + community replication
- **Proven:** DocumentCloud's Annotation + fact-checking workflows
- **Lesson:** Human judgment always required

### Technology Trends
- **Emerging:** Graph databases (Neo4j) becoming standard
- **Proven:** Network visualization critical for relationship investigation
- **Caution:** AI for first-pass flagging only; humans for verification
- **Future:** Privacy-first, local-first tools for high-risk journalists

### Geographic Coverage
- **Well-served:** Western conflict zones, wealthy nations
- **Underserved:** Sub-Saharan Africa, Central Asia, Southeast Asia
- **Opportunity:** Design for Global South from day one

## PAIN POINTS TO ADDRESS

### #1: Tool Fragmentation
Current: Journalists use 5-10+ tools that don't integrate
Solution: Project Truth orchestrates multiple tools in single workflow

### #2: Data Access Restrictions
Current: Social media APIs locked down, paywalled sources
Solution: Aggregate data sources (with legal frameworks)

### #3: Skills Gap
Current: Tools require increasing technical expertise
Solution: Training infrastructure (like Bellingcat model)

### #4: Verification Burden
Current: Manual verification time-consuming
Solution: Human + AI hybrid (AI flags, humans verify)

### #5: Collaboration at Scale
Current: Multi-country investigations hard to coordinate
Solution: Platform designed for consortium-style collaboration

## VERIFICATION SYSTEM OPPORTUNITY

**Gap:** DocumentCloud does annotation well but not relationships. Aleph does relationships but not annotation. Neither has integrated peer review.

**Opportunity:** Design system combining:
1. Annotation (like DocumentCloud)
2. Network relationships (like Aleph/Neo4j)
3. Multi-layer verification (like ICIJ)
4. Community peer review (like Bellingcat)
5. Verification badges (official/journalist/community/unverified)
6. Provenance tracking (who, when, source)
7. Transparent methodology

**This would be unique competitive advantage.**
